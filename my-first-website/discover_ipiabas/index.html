Discover Ipiabas

Known as the Pearl of the Valley

Breathtaking Nature

Atlantic forest, waterfalls, panoramic views and wild animals!

A region surrounded by the Atlantic Forest, in the middle of the Paraiba Valley. You can see unique wild animals and waterfalls.

Local Tasty Food

Made with fresh ingredients from the local family farmers!

Ipiabas has a wide variety of restaurants, and the hightlights are the wood fire food and the tilapia fish plate.

Historical Attractions

From past glorious times!

There are historical atractions to visit, from the old steam train to farm houses from the golden age of coffee plantation.

About Us

From a Family Farmhouse to a Welcoming Pousada

Location

Our lovely pousada is located at 38 Aloisio Trindade Moura Street, Ipiabas, Rio de Janeiro - Brazil.

Ipiabas is a charming small town located just a two-and-a-half-hour drive from Rio de Janeiro. Nestled among rolling hills and the lush Atlantic Forest, it offers a serene escape into nature. The locals are incredibly welcoming, and the cuisine is simply delightful.

Our Pousada

Award winning Pousada in the region!

Our Pousada has been honored with awards from Tripadvisor and Booking.com, thanks to the wonderful reviews from our valued guests. We sincerely thank each and every one of you for your support and appreciation!

Our History

We are the Sano Family owners of Sitio Ipiabas, lovely to meet you here!

We opened Sitio Ipiabas Pousada in 2015. Originally, it was a cozy farmhouse that belonged to our grandfather, who deeply loved the area and the town. After his passing in 2009, we began renovations four years later to create our first rooms, transforming the property into the beloved Pousada it is today.
